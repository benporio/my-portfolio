ABOUT ME

I am a graduate of Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering, and also a board passer on October 2015. My first and long career experience for about 4 years was in Media/Broadcasting Industry as a Broadcasting Technician and suddenly realized that I have to be more than who I am.

Then I found myself having an interest with Programming when my girlfriend asked me for help to resolve the issues they were having with their payroll system created in Excel. I had to learn the scripting language used to create macros and automation in Excel in order to resolve those issues that are critical for their work flow.

Visual Basic for Application (VBA) is the first language I had learned but due to my limited knowledge about how coding should be done, I had just created a messy and unmanageable code. That's the point where I desired to understand the fundamentals and principles more which leads me to learn Object Oriented Programming and other concepts like design patterns and frameworks.

When it comes to being a Programmer, or should I say an Aspiring and Learning Web Developer, I started this in a startup company where my older brother works in. I started to get exposed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using jQuery in this company by creating modules and addons for their web portal product. I have some git repos for these but I know there are lots of mess going around those codes. But I only work as freelance programmer in their company.

Eventually I realized that I need some passive income, this pushes me to find a full time job, and I ended up working as Junior Java Programmer in a company inside Clark Freeport Zone, Pampanga, Zgetcare Systems Inc. Their products focuses on health care system with technology stacks: JSF, Vue, and Java Springboot and JBoss Seam framework.
